do you accept all major credit cards?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard and American Express.

what is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you may return your item(s) within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, in their original condition, and returned with all original packaging. Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed to your original method of payment within a few business days. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a faulty or incorrect item. If you wish to initiate a return or exchange, please contact our support team with your order details, and we will guide you through the process. We are committed to making your shopping experience as smooth and hassle-free as possible..

when will I receive my order?

Once your order has been placed, it will be processed within 1–2 business days. After dispatch, delivery times may vary depending on your location, but most orders are delivered within 3–5 business days. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information as soon as your order has been shipped, allowing you to follow its progress. Please note that delivery times may be slightly longer during busy periods, weekends, or public holidays. If you have not received your order within the expected timeframe, please contact our support team and we will be happy to assist you..
